Transport facilities :

It is actually on the main route from Rajapalayam to Tirunelveli.  Hence frequent bus services are in abundance.  It is nearly 30 Kilometers away from Rajapalayam,
which is in the North,while Tirunelveli is situated at a distance of 55 kilometers in the South.Kovilpatti, a leading marketing centre is at a distance of 40 Kilometers in the East.It is also linked by the Railway 
line, as it lies enroute from senkottai to Madurai and Chennai Railway time.
